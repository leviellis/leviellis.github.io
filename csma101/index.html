CMSA 101 - Introduction to Programming

Musical visualization of Google Trends data created using p5.js

- Each search term is represented by a different instrument associated with a color: blue/cello, red/piano, and yellow/violin

- Notes on the left represent low search term popularity while higher notes on the right indicate increased popularity

- One note is equivalent to an hour, with the corresponding date and time shown on the left
