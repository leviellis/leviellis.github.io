L E V I   E L L I S ABOUT RESUME
My name is Levi Ellis and I am a media composer from Los Angeles, California. I study Computer Science with a focus in Media Arts at Woodbury University as a 4th year honors student. I have been composing and producing music for over a decade, creating music for both vocal artists and media projects including film, video games, audio-visual experiences, and more. I have experience working with game engines and editing suites in addition to digital audio workstations and am equipped to prepare traditional or adaptive musical scores for nearly any digital medium.
