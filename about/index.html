L E V I   E L L I S ABOUT RESUME
Levi Ellis is a composer and creative developer who creates music and designs both interactive and adaptive systems for media projects including video games, film, and other audio-visual experiences. He has partnered with creatives from various game and film production studios including Netflix and Odd Orca Studios to compose and produce musical scores and program audio systems. His work has been showcased in over a dozen film festivals including the Tokyo Film Awards [Silver for Best Composer], NCFA [Best Original Music Nominee], Flick Fair, Fine Cut, and SFiFF, and been featured in video games selected for Indie Game of the Week and GMTK Top 50 Games.
