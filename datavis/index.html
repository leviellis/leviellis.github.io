My term project is a web-based data visualization tool that allows users to compare the popularity of topical social justice-related search terms across different regional demographics within the United States. As it is developed so far, my program is a functional prototype based on projects from my computer science major that processes search data from Google compressed into CSV files. This map shows the popularity percentage of the search term "black lives matter" during the year 2021.
